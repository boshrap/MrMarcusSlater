Role

Norton 360 for Gamers was a re-skin of the Norton 360 app. It’s only difference was the addition of “game boosting” technology borrowed from the acquisition of Bullguard. Essentially, it implemented a few system power settings and other software based enhancements to stabilize gaming performance. My role was to create copy for the new features in the app. The UI copy and and microcopy was part of this, but I also put together feature descriptions and marketing taglines for use across Norton (including the website.) Part of the challenge was legal limitations. There was not significant enough of an effect legally say something really compelling like “improves your computer’s performance while gaming for more FPS.” That said, it did provide some useful features and a way to sell anti-virus to a new audience.
