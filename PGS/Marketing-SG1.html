Petrol Advertising – Seagate Barracuda Re-Launch
Role

I was contracted by Petrol Advertising to help them create content with Seagate for the reintroduction of their storied Barracuda brand of hard-drives. They requested copy for the catalog and ecommerce pages that was written in line with their desired content guidelines.

There were several phases: 1) I participated in the kick-off meeting with the client to help determine the deliverables for the project, which were fairly straightforward. 2) I first worked on the creating the basic copy and descriptions for the various sub-brands of barracuda. I created tripartite slogans for each brand complimenting the animal-elemental hybrid theme Seagate had committed to. 3) Then, I created layouts for the A+ content using amazon’s templates in Word. I created a few drafts and reviewed with the resident copywriter before sending the final content. 4) Lastly, I wrote copy for each section of the catalog using the existing catalog as reference.

Ultimately the contract ended and Seagate’s Barracuda brand launched to great success. The Amazon web content was originally used as I sent it, but the templates have been updated to a different design. The copy, however, is the largely what I wrote.

The tripartite slogans remain even today (2024!) To me, that is a good sign that our campaign was successful.
