Online Access App – iOS/Android Update Matrix
Role

The Edward Jones app had been through several iterations, and an upcoming update would’ve been incompatible with various user’s devices.

To address this, I worked with the mobile device team to create a content matrix of messages for users with different devices and updates. After some discussion, we managed to suss out all of the required error cases and create content for each. This is not the exact copy, but it is similar:

Warning Post-Deadline
Device obsolete
App obsolete

To keep using this app after 12/24, you must switch to a device on the compatibility list, and update your app.

You can always access your accounts through your browser on Edward Jones Online Access.

Your device is no longer compatible with the Online Access app. Please switch to a device on the compatibility list to use this app.

You can always access your accounts through your browser on Edward Jones Online Access.
OS obsolete
App obsolete
Device OK

To continue using this app after 12/24, you must update your device’s OS and your app first.

You can always access your accounts through your browser on Edward Jones Online Access.

Your device OS and app need to be updated before you can continue.

You can always access your accounts through your browser on Edward Jones Online Access.
App obsolete
Device OK
OS OK

You must update your app in order to keep using Edward Jones Online Access.

You can always access your accounts through your browser on Edward Jones Online Access.

You need to update your before you can continue.

You can always access your accounts through your browser on Edward Jones Online Access.

I created a more sophisticated version of the matrix above with specifc copy for different versions of iOS and Andorid with specific CTA buttons for both. The content was then vetted by the legal department and pushed to users' devices a month before the update deadline.
