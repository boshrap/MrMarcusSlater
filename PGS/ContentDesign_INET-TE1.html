Role

Our team was asked to create strategy to capitalize on the technical content in forums by creating new content. Each editor presented a strategy and mine was selected. I created five content categories that were consistent across Internet Brands car forums, so that it would be scalable. Then, we used analytics data (from the forums themselves, but also Google) to prioritize article topics by popularity and SERP rank. Then I created a step-by-step work flow for creating blog content in each of the categories by means of sample articles. I then used those articles as templates to develop assignments for freelance writers and other editors.

After the results of the first phase of content proved the concept, we hired another 3 editors and content manger to increase the volume of articles. The value add to advertisers and the general bump in traffic from evergreen content was a great success.
