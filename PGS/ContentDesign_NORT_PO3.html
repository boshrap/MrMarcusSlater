Norton Portal - Restricted Persons Flow
Role

The law requires that Norton deny service to entities that are on the governmentâ€™s restricted persons list. This project was to develop a flow that catches new and existing restricted persons, notifies them, and restricts access (if necessary.)

The flow was triggered at several points in different flows: at login, for existing users who were newly restricted; during the creating of new accounts; for users who enter personal information that matches a restricted person; and when updating account information, for users whose update their info to match that of a restricted party.

This project was initiated by the legal department, and both flows and copy were developed with their approval. There were some challenges with the logic of triggering the alerts (particularly regarding updates,) but we were able to create a compliant UI that was verified as clear and unconfusing through user testing.
