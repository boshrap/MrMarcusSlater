Norton Portal - "Delete My Data" GDPR Flow
Role

This was a project that originated from the legal department, and required significant review from stakeholders on the product management side as well. The flow can be triggered from the userâ€™s settings page on both the mobile and web applications. My role was to create the copy for each screen of the flow; consider and discuss the intention of the user; and to discourage any accidental cancellations. The copy and flow for this in general was fairly high-profile and subject to many (productive!) discussions between myself, product managers, and the legal department.

The design and copy went through multiple rounds of user testing and revisions before arriving at the final version. There are significant copy warnings and intentional design decisions (like the cancel button) to help prevent accidental account deletions.
