Role

I was brought into an ongoing project where the objective was to adapt a new content system to the setting screens in the Online Access web and mobile apps. While the design and information-architecture were already finished, the pages themselves had not been reviewed by an editor.

I started by interviewing the project designer and manager, to learn the flow and then settled on a schedule to review each section with special care to the Edward Jones Texting and Alerts sections. These screen held the users contact information and guided them through the process of enabling the service (including the terms and conditions). I left Figma comments regarding labels, instruction, style concerns, microcontent, and questions.

All revisions were confirmed after being recorded and discussed with the legal department before being implemented in the app.
